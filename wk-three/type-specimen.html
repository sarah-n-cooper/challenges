Courier

You Make Me Feel Like A Natural Woman

Courier is a monospaced slab serif typeface. Designed by Bud Kettler in 1955, and redrawn by Adrian Frutiger, it has become a standard font for many industries including for screenplays and the US State Department. Originally designed for use with strike on typewriters, Courier has found renewed relevancy in the electronic world in situations where columns of characters must be consistently aligned.
